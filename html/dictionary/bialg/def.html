Definitions Concerning Coalgebras

Definition of Bialgebras

Let
  • $\mathbb{K}$: a field.
A bialgebra over $\mathbb{K}$ or a $\mathbb{K}$-bialgebra is a vector space $B$ over $\mathbb{K}$ which is an algebra and a coalgebra such that the multiplication and unit are coalgebra homomorphisms, or equivalently, the comultiplication and counit are algebra homomorphisms.
