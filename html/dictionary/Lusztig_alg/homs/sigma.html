Anti-Algebra Involutions $\sigma$ on Lusztig Algebras

Definition

Let
  • $I$: a Cartan datum.
  • $\mathbf{f}$: the Lusztig algebra.
Then, there exists a unique anti-algebra involution $\sigma$ on $\mathbf{f}$ such that \[ \sigma(\theta_i) = \theta_i \text{ for all } i \in I. \]([Lus10, Section 1.2.9]).
