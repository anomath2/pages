Cartan Data of Type $E$

Definition

Let
  • $n \in \{ 6,\ 7,\ 8 \}$.
The Cartan datum of type $E_n$ is the Cartan datum $I = \{ 1,2,\dots,n \}$ such that \[ i \cdot j = \begin{cases} 2 & \text{ if } i = j, \\ -1 & \text{ if } |i-j| = 1 \text{ and } i,j \geq 3, \\ & \text{ or } (i,j) \in \{ (1,3),\ (3,1),\ (2,4),\ (4,2) \}, \\ 0 & \text{ otherwise} \end{cases} \ \text{ for all } i,j \in I. \]
A Cartan datum is said to be of type $E$ if it is proportional to the Cartan datum of type $E_n$.
