Cartan Data of Type $F$

Definition

The Cartan datum of type $F_4$, or of type $F$, is the Cartan datum $I = \{ 1,2,3,4 \}$ such that \[ i \cdot j = \begin{cases} 2 & \text{ if } i = j, \\ -1 & \text{ if } |i-j| = 1 \text{ and } (i,j) \neq (3,2), \\ -2 & \text{ or } (i,j) = (3,2), \\ 0 & \text{ otherwise} \end{cases} \ \text{ for all } i,j \in I. \]
A Cartan datum is said to be of type $F$ if it is proportional to the Cartan datum of type $F_4$.
