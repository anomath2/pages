Properties of Cartan Data

Characterization of Irreducibility

Let
  • $I$: a Cartan datum.
The following are equivalent.
  • $I$ is irreducible.
  • The Coxeter graph of $I$ is connected.

Characterization of Finite Types

Let
  • $I$: a Cartan datum.
Then, the following are equivalent.
  • $I$ is of finite type.
  • The Cartan matrix $(a_{i,j})_{i,j \in I}$ of $I$ is of finite type (as a generalized Cartan matrix).
  • The Weyl group of $I$ is a finite group.

Classification of Finite Types

Let
  • $I$: an irreducible Cartan datum of finite type.
Then, it is proportional to one of the Cartan data of type $A_n$, $B_n$, $C_n$, $D_n$, $E_n$, $F_4$, and $G_2$.
