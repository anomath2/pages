Examples of Coalgebras

Coordinate Coalgebra of $M_n(\mathbb{K})$

Let
  • $\mathbb{K}$: a field.
  • $n \in \mathbb{Z}_{> 0}$.
  • $M_n(\mathbb{K})$: the algebra of $(n \times n)$-matrices.
For each $1 \leq i,j \leq n$, let $u_j^i$ denote the linear form on $M_n(\mathbb{K})$ defined by \[ u_j^i(X) = x_{i,j} \text{ for all } X = (x_{i,j})_{1 \leq i,j \leq n} \in M_n(\mathbb{K}). \]Set \[ \mathbb{K}[M_n(\mathbb{K})] := \operatorname{Span}_\mathbb{K} \{ u_j^i \mid 1 leq i,j \leq n \}. \]Then, it is a coalgebra with comultiplication \[ \Delta(u_j^i) := \sum_{k=1}^n u_k^i \otimes u_j^k \]and counit \[ \epsilon(u_j^i) = \delta_{i,j}. \]
