2. Compact Quantum Groups

Let $G = U(n)$, $0 < q < 1$, $n \geq 2$. We identify $\mathfrak{g}_\mathbb{C}$ with $\mathfrak{gl}(n,\mathbb{C})$. Let $\mathcal{U}_q$ and $\mathcal{A}_q$ denote the quantized enveloping algebra and the quantized coordinate ring, respectively.
For each $\lambda \in P^+$, let $W(\lambda) \subset \mathcal{A}_q$ denote the subspace spanned by the matrix coefficients for $V(\lambda)$. Then one has the following multiplicity-free decomposition of the $\mathcal{U}_q$-bimodule: \[ \mathcal{A}_q = \bigoplus_{\lambda \in P^+} W(\lambda), \quad W(\lambda) \simeq V(\lambda) \otimes V(\lambda)^*. \]
