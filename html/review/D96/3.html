3. Quantum Homogeneous Spaces

The "infinitesimal" method to construct quantum homogeneous spaces uses the idea that the algebra of functions on the homogeneous space $G/K$ can also be defined as the subspace of those functions on $G$ which are annihilated by the $G$-invariant differential operators $X \in \mathfrak{k}_\mathbb{C} \subset \mathfrak{g}_\mathbb{C}$.
