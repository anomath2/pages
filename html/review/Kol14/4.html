4. Quantum involutions

4.1. The case $X = \emptyset$

Define an algebra automorphism $\psi$ on $U_q(\mathfrak{g})$ by \[ \psi(E_i) = E_i K_i, \quad \psi(F_i) = K_i^{-1} F_i, \quad \psi(K_h) = K_h. \]

4.2. A $q$-analog of $\operatorname{Ad}(m_X)$

Define \[ T_X := T_{w_X} \circ \psi: U_q(\mathfrak{g}) \rightarrow U_q(\mathfrak{g}). \]

Definition 4.3

Set \[ \theta_q(X,\tau) := s(X,\tau) \circ T_X \circ \tau \circ \omega : U_q(\mathfrak{g}') \rightarrow U_q(\mathfrak{g}'). \]

4.5. References to Letzter's constructions

  • For finite dimensional $\mathfrak{g}$, an automorphism $\tilde{\theta}_2$ very similar to $\theta_q$ was constructed in [Let99, Thereom 3.1].
