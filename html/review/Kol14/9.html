9. Equivalence of coideal subalgebras

Assumption

We consider over $\overline{\mathbb{K}(q)}$.

Definition 9.1

Let $C,D$ be right coideal subalgebras of a Hopf algebra $H$. We say that $C$ is equivalent to $D$ if there exists a Hopf algebra automorphism $\phi$ on $H$ such that \[ \phi(C) = D. \]In this case, we write \[ C \sim D. \]

Let $B,B'$ be coideal subalgebras of $U_q(\mathfrak{g}')$. We write \[ B \overset{\tilde{H}}{\sim} B' \]if there exists $x \in \tilde{H}$ such that \[ x B = B'. \]

For each $\mathbf{s},\mathbf{s}' \in \overline{\mathcal{S}}$, we write \[ \mathbf{s} \overset{\mathcal{S}}{\sim} \mathbf{s}' \]if \[ s_i = \pm s'_i \text{ for all } i \in I_{\mathrm{ns}}. \]

Proposition 9.2

  • (1) Let $\mathbf{c} \in \overline{\mathcal{C}}$ and $\mathbf{s} \in \overline{\mathcal{S}}$. Then, there exist $\mathbf{d} \in \overline{\mathcal{D}}$ and $\mathbf{s}' \in \overline{\mathcal{S}}$ such that \[ B_{\mathbf{c},\mathbf{s}} \overset{\tilde{H}}{\sim} B_{\mathbf{d}, \mathbf{s}'}. \]
  • (2) Let $\mathbf{d},\mathbf{d}' \in \overline{\mathcal{D}}$ and $\mathbf{s},\mathbf{s}' \in \overline{\mathcal{S}}$. Then $B_{\mathbf{d},\mathbf{s}} \overset{\tilde{H}}{\sim} B_{\mathbf{d}',\mathbf{s}'}$ if and only if $\mathbf{d} = \mathbf{d}'$ and $\mathbf{s} \overset{\mathcal{S}}{\sim} \mathbf{s}'$.

Theorem 9.7

There is a one-to-one correspondence between the equivalence classes of quantum symmetric pair coideal subalgebras of $U_q(\mathfrak{g}')$ corresponding to the admissible pair $(X, \tau)$ and the $\operatorname{Aut}(A,X)^\tau$-orbits in $\overline{\mathcal{D}} \times (\overline{\mathcal{S}}/\overset{\mathcal{S}}{\sim})$.
