6. Triangular decompositions

6.1. A one-sided $U^+ U^{0 \prime}$-module basis of $U_q(\mathfrak{g}')$

Fix $\mathcal{J} \subset \bigcup_{n \geq 0} I^n$ such that \[ \{ F_J \mid J \in \mathcal{J} \} \]forms a basis of $U^-$.

Proposition 6.1

The set \[ \{ B_J \mid J \in \mathcal{J} \} \]is a basis of the left $U^+ U^{0 \prime}$-module $U_q(\mathfrak{g}')$.

Proposition 6.2

The set \[ \{ B_J \mid J \in \mathcal{J} \} \]is a basis of the left $\mathcal{M}_X^+ U_\Theta^{0 \prime}$-module $B_{\mathbf{c},\mathbf{s}}$.
