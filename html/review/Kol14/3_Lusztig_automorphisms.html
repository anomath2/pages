3. Lusztig automorphisms

3.1. Quantum groups

The Hopf algebra structure of $U_q(\mathfrak{g})$ is given as follows. \begin{align*} \begin{split} &\Delta(E_i) = E_i \otimes 1 + K_i \otimes E_i, \quad \epsilon(E_i) = 0, \quad S(E_i) = -K_i^{-1} E_i, \\ &\Delta(F_i) = F_i \otimes K_i^{-1} + 1 \otimes F_i, \quad \epsilon(F_i) = 0, \quad S(F_i) = -F_i K_i, \\ &\Delta(K_h) = K_h \otimes K_h, \quad \epsilon(K_h) = 1, \quad S(K_h) = K_{-h}. \end{split} \end{align*}
$x \in \tilde{H}$ acts on $U_q(\mathfrak{g})$ by \[ x u = x(\beta) u \text{ for all } u \in U_q(\mathfrak{g})_\beta,\ \beta \in Q. \]
