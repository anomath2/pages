Abstract

  • Drinfeld and Jimbo defined the quantized enveloping algebras $\mathbf{U}$ by generators and relations.
  • The definition of $\mathbf{U}$ in this book is somewhat different. The quantum Serre relations are automatically deduced.
  • The universal $R$-matrix was introduced by Drinfeld as an intertwiner between the comultiplication $\Delta$ of $\mathbf{U}$ and its transpose.
  • The quasi-$R$-matrix is an intertwiner between $\Delta$ and $\overline{\Delta}$, the $Delta$ conjugated by the bar-involution.
  • The higher order quantum Serre relations are used in the discussion of the quantum Frobenius homomorphisms and of the braid group actions.
