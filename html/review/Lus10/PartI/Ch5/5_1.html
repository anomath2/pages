5.1. The Category $\mathcal{C}'_i$

5.1.1

In this chapter, we fix $i \in I$.
Let $\mathcal{C}'_i$ denote the category whose objects are $\mathbb{Z}$-graded $\mathbb{Q}(v)$-vector space $M = \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{Z}} M^n$ provided with two locally nilpotent linear endomorphisms $E_i, F_i$ such that
  • (a) $E_i M^n \subset M^{n+2}$, $F_i M^n \subset M^{n-2}$ for all $n \in \mathbb{Z}$.
  • (b) $[E_i, F_i]$ is multiplication by $[n]_i$ for all $n \in \mathbb{Z}$.
The morphisms in $\mathcal{C}'_i$ are linear maps preserving the $\mathbb{Z}$-gradings and commuting with $E_i$, $F_i$.

5.1.2

For $M \in \mathcal{C}'_i$, let $c$ denote the endomorphism on $M$ given by \[ c(m) := \left( E_i F_i + \frac{v_i^{n-1} + v_i^{-n+1} - 2}{(v_i - v_i^{-1})^2} \right) m = \left( F_i E_i + \frac{v_i^{n+1} + v_i^{-n-1}-2}{(v_i-v_i^{-1})^2} \right)m \text{ for all } m \in M^n. \]
For $n \in \mathbb{Z}$, set \[ s_n := \frac{v_i^{n+1} + v_i^{-n-1}-2}{(v_i-v_i^{-1})^2}, \quad s'_n := \frac{v_i^{n-1} + v_i^{-n+1} - 2}{(v_i - v_i^{-1})^2}. \]

Proposition 5.1.3

Let $n \in \mathbb{Z}$ and $t \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}$.
  • (a) The subspace \[ M^n(t) := \{ m \in M^n \mid E_i^{(t)}m = 0 \} \] is $c$-stable and \[ (c-s_n)(c-s_{n+2}) \cdots (c-s_{n+2t}) = 0 \text{ on } M^n(t). \]
  • (b) The subspace \[ M^n[t] := \{ m \in M^n \mid F_i^{(t)}m = 0 \} \] is $c$-stable and \[ (c-s'_n)(c-s'_{n-2}) \cdots (c-s'_{n-2t}) = 0 \text{ on } M^n[t]. \]
  • (c) The map $c$ is a locally finite, semisimple map.
