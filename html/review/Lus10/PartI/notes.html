Notes

  • The simplest case (quantum analogue of $SL_2$) was defined by Kulish and Reshetikhin [KL81].
  • The general case was defined by Drinfeld [Dr85] and Jimbo [Jim85], [Jim86].
  • The definition is due to Drinfeld [Dr86].
  • A characterization of the quasi-$R$-matrix is given by Lusztig [Lus92].
