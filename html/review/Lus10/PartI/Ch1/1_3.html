1.3. Preliminaries on Gaussian Binomial Coefficients

1.3.1

Set \[ \mathcal{A} := \mathbb{Z}[v,v^{-1}]. \]
For $a \in \mathbb{Z}$ and $t \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}$, \[ {a \brack t} := \prod_{s=1}^t \frac{v^{a-s+1} - v^{-a+s-1}}{v^s - v^{-s}} \in \mathcal{A}. \]

1.3.3

For $n \in \mathbb{Z}$, \[ [n] := {n \brack 1} = \frac{v^n - v^{-n}}{v - v^{-1}}, \quad [n]! := \prod_{s=1}^n [s]. \]
If $a,t \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}$ and $t \leq a$, then \[ {a \brack t} = \frac{[a]!}{[t]![a-t]!}. \]
