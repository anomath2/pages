24.1. Integrability Properties of the Quasi-$R$-Matrix

24.1.1

Let $M,M' \in \mathcal{C}$ be such that either ${}^\omega M \in \mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{hi}}$ or $M' \in \mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{hi}}$. Then, the quasi-$R$-matrix $\Theta$ acts on $M \otimes M'$.

Lemma 24.1.2

Let $M,M'$ be as above. Then,
  • (a) $\Delta(u) \Theta(m \otimes m') = \Theta(\overline{\Delta}(u)(m \otimes m'))$ for all $m \in M$, $m' \in M'$.

Corollary 24.1.5

Assume that the root datum is $Y$-regular. Let $\lambda,\lambda' \in X^+$. The map $\Theta$ leaves ${}^\omega_\mathcal{A} \Lambda_\lambda \otimes {}_\mathcal{A} \Lambda_{\lambda'}$.
