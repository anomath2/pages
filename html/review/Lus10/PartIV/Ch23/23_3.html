23.3. $\dot{\mathbf{U}}$ and Tensor Products

Proposition 23.3.10

Let $M \in \mathcal{C}'$ and $m \in M^\zeta$. Then, there exist $\lambda,\lambda' \in X^+$ and a morphism $f : {}^\omega \Lambda_\lambda \otimes \Lambda_{\lambda'} \rightarrow M$ in $\mathcal{C}'$ such that \[ \lambda'-\lambda = \zeta, \quad f(\xi_{-\lambda} \otimes \eta_{\lambda'}) = m. \]
