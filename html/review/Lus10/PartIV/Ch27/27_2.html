27.2. The Subsets $B[\lambda]$

Assumption

The Cartan datum is of finite type.

27.2.1

Let $(M,B) \in \tilde{\mathcal{C}}$ and $b \in B$. There exists a unique $\lambda \in X^+$ such that $b \in M[\geq \lambda]$ and $\lambda$ is maximal with this property.
Let $B[\lambda]$ denote the set of $b \in B$ which give rise to $\lambda \in X^+$ as above. Then, \[ B = \bigsqcup_{\lambda \in X^+} B[\lambda]. \]
For each $\lambda \in X^+$, \[ (M[\geq \lambda], \bigsqcup_{\lambda' \geq \lambda} B[\lambda']), \quad (M[> \lambda], \bigsqcup_{\lambda' > \lambda} B[\lambda']) \]are based modules.
