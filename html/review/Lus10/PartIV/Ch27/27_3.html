27.3. Tensor Product of Based Modules

Assumption

The Cartan datum is of finite type.

27.3.1

Let $(M,B), (M',B') \in \tilde{\mathcal{C}}$. Set \[ \Psi := \Theta \circ \overline{\cdot}^{\otimes 2}. \]
Let $\mathcal{L}$ denote the $\mathbb{Z}[v^{-1}]$-submodule of $M \otimes M'$ generated by $B \otimes B'$.

Theorem 27.3.2

  • (a) For each $(b_1,b'_1) \in B \times B'$, there exists a unique element $b_1 \diamondsuit b'_1 \in \mathcal{L}$ such that \[ \Psi(b_1 \diamondsuit b'_1) = b_1 \diamondsuit b'_1, \quad b_1 \diamondsuit b'_1 - b_1 \otimes b'_1 \in v^{-1} \mathcal{L}. \]
  • (c) $\{ b_1 \diamondsuit b'_1 \mid (b_1,b'_1) \in B \times B' \}$ forms a basis of $\mathcal{L}$.
