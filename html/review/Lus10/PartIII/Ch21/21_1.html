21.1.1

Assume that the Cartan datum is of finite type.
For $\lambda \in X^+$, set \[ \lambda' := -w_0(\lambda) \in X^+. \]The canonical basis $\mathbf{B}(\Lambda_{\lambda'})$ of $\Lambda_{\lambda'}$ may be regarded as a basis of ${}^\omega \Lambda_{\lambda'}$.

Proposition 21.1.2

There exists a unique isomorphism of $\mathbf{U}$-modules \[ \chi: \Lambda_\lambda \rightarrow {}^\omega \Lambda_{\lambda'} \]such that \[ \chi(\mathbf{B}(\Lambda_\lambda)) = \mathbf{B}(\Lambda_{\lambda'}). \]
