18.1. Preliminaries

Assumption

In this chapter, we assume that the root datum is $Y$-regular, and $\lambda \in X^+$ is fixed.

18.1.5

For each $\nu \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}[I]$, set \[ \mathcal{L}(\mathbf{f})_\nu := \mathbb{Z}[v^{-1}] \mathcal{B}_\nu. \]Then, \[ \mathcal{L}(\mathbf{f}) = \bigoplus_{\nu \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}[I]} \mathcal{L}(\mathbf{f})_\nu. \]

Proposition 18.1.7

Let $\nu \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}[I]$.
  • (a) $\mathcal{L}(\mathbf{f})_\nu$ coincides with the $\mathbb{Z}[v^{-1}]$-submodule of $\mathbf{f}$ generated by the elements \[ \tilde{\phi}_{i_i} \tilde{\phi}_{i_2} \cdots \tilde{\phi}_{i_r} 1 \] with $i_1 + \cdots i_r = \nu$.
  • (b) The image of $\mathcal{B}_\nu$ in $\mathcal{L}(\mathbf{f})/v^{-1} \mathcal{L}(\mathbf{f})$ coincides with the images of the elements \[ \pm \tilde{\phi}_{i_i} \tilde{\phi}_{i_2} \cdots \tilde{\phi}_{i_r} 1 \] with $i_1 + \cdots i_r = \nu$.
