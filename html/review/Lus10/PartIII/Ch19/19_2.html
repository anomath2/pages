19.2. Normalization of Signs

Assumption

In this chapter, the root datum is $Y$-regular, and $\lambda \in X^+$ is fixed.

19.2.1

For $x \in \mathbf{f}_\nu$, the following are equivalent:
  • (a) $x \in \mathbf{B}_\nu + v^{-1} \mathcal{L}(\mathbf{f})_\nu$.
  • (b) $x = \tilde{\phi}_{i_1} \tilde{\phi}_{i_2} \cdots \tilde{\phi}_{i_r} 1 \pmod{v^{-1} \mathcal{L}(\mathbf{f})_\nu}$ for some $i_1,\dots,i_r \in I$ with $i_1 + \dots + i_r = \nu$.
