20.2. Basis at $\infty$ in a Tensor Product

Theorem 20.2.2

Let $M, M' \in \mathcal{C}'$ with finite-dimensional weight spaces. Assume that $(L, \mathbf{b})$ and $(L', \mathbf{b}')$ are given bases at $\infty$ of $M$ and $M'$, respectively. Then, the free $\mathbf{A}$-submodule $L \otimes_{\mathbf{A}} L'$ of $M \otimes M'$ and the $\mathbb{Q}$-basis $\mathbf{b} \otimes \mathbf{b}'$ of $(L \otimes_{\mathbf{A}} L')/v^{-1}(L \otimes_{\mathbf{A}} L') = (L/v^{-1} L) \otimes_\mathbb{Q} (L'/v^{-1} L')$ define a basis at $\infty$ of $M \otimes M'$.
