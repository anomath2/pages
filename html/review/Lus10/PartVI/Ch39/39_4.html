39.4. Proof of the Braid Relations

Theorem 39.4.3

Let $i \neq j \in I$.
  • (a) $T'_{i,e} T'_{j,e} T'_{i,e} \cdots = T'_{j,e} T'_{i,e} T'_{j,e} \cdots$ on $\mathbf{U}$ and all $M \in \mathcal{C}'$; both hand sides have $h(i,j)$ factors.
  • (b) $T''_{i,e} T''_{j,e} T''_{i,e} \cdots = T''_{j,e} T''_{i,e} T''_{j,e} \cdots$ on $\mathbf{U}$ and all $M \in \mathcal{C}'$; both hand sides have $h(i,j)$ factors.
