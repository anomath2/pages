[D05] 2.4. Fourier Series Revisited

The space $C(\mathbb{R}/\mathbb{Z})$ is a pre-Hilbert space, but not complete. Let $L^2(\mathbb{R}/\mathbb{Z})$ denote its completion.

Theorem 2.4.1

The exponentials $(e_k)_{k \in \mathbb{Z}}$ form an orthonormal basis of the Hilbert space $L^2(\mathbb{R}/\mathbb{Z})$.
