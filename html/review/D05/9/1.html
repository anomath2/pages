9.1. Matrix Groups

Define a norm on $\operatorname{Mat}_n(\mathbb{C})$ by \[ ||A||_1 := \sum_{i,j=1}^n |a_{i,j}|. \]

Proposition 9.1.2

The subspace $GL_n(\mathbb{C}) \subset \operatorname{Mat}_n(\mathbb{C})$ is an LC group.

Lemma 9.1.3

The unitary group \[ U(n) := \{ g \in \operatorname{Mat}_n(\mathbb{C}) \mid g^* g = 1 \} \]is a compact subgroup of $GL_n(\mathbb{C})$.
