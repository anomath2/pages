8.4. Plancherel's Theorem

Theorem 8.4.2

Let $A$ be an LCA group. There is a unique Haar measure on $\hat{A}$ such that for every $f \in L^1_\mathrm{bc}(A)$ the Fourier transform $\hat{f}$ lies in $L^2_\mathrm{bc}(\hat{A})$, and \[ ||f||_2 = ||\hat{f}||_2. \]The Fourier transform extends to an isomorphism \[ L^2(A) \rightarrow L^2(\hat{A}) \]of Hilbert spaces.
