Quantum zonal spherical functions and Macdonald polynomials

Abstract

A unified theory of quantum symmetric pair is applied to $q$-special functions. Certain left coideal subalgebras in the quantized enveloping algebra establishes an appropriate framework for quantum zonal spherical functions. Here a distinguished family of such functions is shown to be a family of Macdonald polynomials, as conjectured by Koornwinder and Macdonald.
